
Common Name: Bell Pepper
Scientific Name: Capsicum annuum
Family: Solanaceae
Cultivation: Full sun, well-drained soil, regular watering.
Propagation: Seeds, seedlings.
Planting: Spring, 18-24 inches apart.
Care: Support for heavy fruiting, fertilize during growth.
Harvesting: When peppers reach desired size and color (green, yellow, red).
