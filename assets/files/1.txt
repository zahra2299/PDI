
Common Name: Tomato
Scientific Name: Solanum lycopersicum
Family: Solanaceae
Cultivation: Full sun, well-draining soil, regular watering.
Propagation: Seeds, seedlings.
Planting: Spring, 18-24 inches apart.
Care: Prune for airflow, fertilize during growth.
Harvesting: 60-85 days, when fruit is firm and red.
